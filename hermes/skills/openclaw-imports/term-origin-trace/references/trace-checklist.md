# Term Origin Trace Checklist

## Fast checklist

Before finalizing, confirm:

- Do I distinguish prehistory / naming / popularization / mainstreaming?
- Do I separate “similar practice existed” from “exact term existed”?
- Do I know which claims are primary-source backed?
- Do I explain why the term matters now, not just when it appeared?
- Is the closing summary sharper than the body?
- Do I explicitly include **术语溯源 / 关键时间线 / 各大公司或人物的核心观点 / 核心争议 / 一句话总结**?
- Would this read like a forwardable industry note rather than a raw research memo?

## Minimal evidence pack

For most terms, aim to gather:

1. 1-2 primary sources for the earliest explicit naming in the target sense
2. 1 source showing public spread or influential reframing
3. 1-3 sources from other credible actors showing mainstream adoption
4. 1 source or argument capturing the main criticism / boundary dispute

## Recommended phrasing

### When evidence is strong

- “目前可确认的早期公开记录显示……”
- “在 AI Agent 语境下，X 是较早明确使用该词的公开来源之一。”
- “真正推动其行业化传播的是 Y，而不只是 X 的首次命名。”

### When evidence is mixed

- “如果严格区分前史、命名与普及，答案并不是同一个人。”
- “相关实践更早存在，但这个词在当前语境下的显性化发生在……”
- “更稳妥的说法是：Y 命名并引爆了它，而不是凭空发明了它。”

## Executive compression template

> 这个概念通常不是被某个人发明的，而是经历了“前史实践已存在—少数人显性命名—关键人物引爆传播—行业逐步定型”这条路径。对管理者而言，重要的不是术语考据本身，而是它背后把什么工作对象、系统边界和组织动作重新说清楚了。

## Social thread template

- 1 opening sentence with the punchline
- 1 short prehistory paragraph
- 3-6 timeline bullets
- 3-5 actor viewpoints
- 1 critique / boundary paragraph
- 1 one-sentence summary
